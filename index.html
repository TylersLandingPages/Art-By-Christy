01 / Statement

On the practice

I paint to slow the world down. Each piece begins with a single color held against the dark, and grows outward from there — petal, feather, scale, spark — until the canvas tells me it's finished.

My work moves between three modes: botanical studies in acrylic, animal portraiture in restrained palettes, and scratchboard abstractions where pattern becomes a kind of meditation. What ties them together is a deep love of ornament and a faith that beauty is worth taking seriously.

Every piece on this site is an original — hand-painted, signed, and available for inquiry. Commissions are accepted on a rolling basis throughout the year.
